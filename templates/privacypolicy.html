Book My Turf - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Book My Turf collects, uses, and protects the personal information of its users.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information, including

  • Contact information (e.g., name, email address, phone number)
  • Booking details (e.g., date, time, turf facility)
  • Payment information (e.g., credit card details) securely processed by our payment service provider

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for purposes such as...

  • Facilitating turf bookings
  • Processing payments
  • Communicating with you about your bookings and our services
  • Improving our website and services

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties.

4. Security Measures

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@bookmyturf.com.
