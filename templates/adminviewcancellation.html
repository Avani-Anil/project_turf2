Book My Turf - Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation Policy:

1.1 Cancellation by Users:
Users may cancel a booking on the Book My Turf platform subject to the following conditions:

  • Full Refund: If the cancellation is made at least 48 hours before the scheduled booking time, users are eligible for a full refund.
  • Partial Refund: Cancellations made within 48 hours of the scheduled booking time may be subject to a partial refund, with the exact amount determined by the policies of the turf facility.

1.2 Cancellation by Turf Facility
In the event that a booked turf becomes unavailable due to unforeseen circumstances or issues on the turf facility's end, users will be notified promptly, and a full refund will be issued.

2. Refund Policy:

2.1 Refund Eligibility:
Refunds will only be processed if the cancellation is made in accordance with the specified timelines mentioned in Section 1.

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during the booking.
  • Certain fees, such as booking fees or service charges, may be non-refundable.
  • Please allow up to 7 business days for the refund to be processed and reflected in the user's account.

3. Contact Information:

For any inquiries or assistance related to cancellations and refunds, please contact our customer support at support@bookmyturf.com.

4. Policy Updates:

Book My Turf reserves the right to update or modify this cancellation and refund policy at any time. Users will be notified of any changes through the platform.
